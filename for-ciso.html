Common questions

What compliance teams ask us

Does using Threadledger guarantee EU AI Act compliance?
No tool can guarantee regulatory compliance — that's a legal determination. What Threadledger gives you is a formally verified, continuously tested, on-chain evidence chain that demonstrates you have implemented a risk management system, technical controls, and audit logging in accordance with Articles 9, 12, and 17. That's what regulators are actually checking for.
Do I need to involve my development team to use this?
L0 (threat modeling) is entirely within the compliance officer's workbench — no developer involvement. L1-L3 are automated from your model. The only developer touchpoint is the IDE agent at L2, which is guidance, not gatekeeping. Compliance defines the rules; the platform enforces them.
What regulations does Threadledger cover today?
EU AI Act, GDPR, NIST AI RMF, and MAS TRM are live today. ISO 42001, PDPA, and Korea AI Act are in the community roadmap. Regulation profiles are modular — you swap them without rebuilding your threat model.
Can I export evidence for a regulator submission?
Yes. Enterprise tier includes regulator submission exports. The on-chain AuditRegistry is IPFS-pinned and exportable in standard formats. CI compliance reports are generated per release and permanently stored.

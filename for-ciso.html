Common questions

What compliance teams ask us

Does using Threadledger guarantee EU AI Act compliance?
No tool can guarantee regulatory compliance — that is a legal determination. What Threadledger gives you is a formally verified, continuously tested, on-chain evidence chain that demonstrates you have implemented a risk management system, technical controls, and audit logging in accordance with Articles 9, 12, and 17. That is what regulators check for.
Do I need to involve my development team to use this?
The risk modeling step is entirely within the compliance officer's workbench — no developer involvement. Controls are generated automatically from your model. The only developer touchpoint is the IDE guidance agent, which coaches developers at coding time. Compliance defines the rules; the platform enforces them.
What regulations does Threadledger cover today?
EU AI Act, GDPR, NIST AI RMF, and MAS TRM are live today. Additional regulation profiles are on the community roadmap. Profiles are modular — you swap them without rebuilding your risk model.
Can I export evidence for a regulator submission?
Yes. Enterprise tier includes regulator submission exports. The on-chain audit log is exportable in standard formats. Compliance reports are generated per release and permanently stored.

# Voiceover Scripts

Four sentences total. Opening and closing for each video.  
Read at 130 words per minute — deliberately slower than natural speech.

---

## CISO & DPOs video

### Opening (3 seconds)
> *"Your AI is live. August 2026 is your deadline. Here's how you prove you're ready."*

Place at: **0:13** in the timeline — just as the logo intro fades to black.

### Closing (3 seconds)
> *"Threadledger. Every claim provable, every check automated, every audit trail permanent."*

Place at: **10 seconds before the end** of the timeline.

---

## DevSecOps video

### Opening (4 seconds)
> *"Compliance is being handed to engineering with no tooling and no definition of done. This is the fix."*

Place at: **0:13** in the timeline.

### Closing (3 seconds)
> *"Threadledger. Your AI can't run any other way."*

Place at: **10 seconds before the end** of the timeline.

---

## Recording notes

- Record each sentence **three times** back to back in Audacity
- Pause 3 seconds between takes — do not stop recording
- Pick the best take in editing, delete the other two
- Export each as WAV: `vo-ciso-open.wav`, `vo-ciso-close.wav`, `vo-devops-open.wav`, `vo-devops-close.wav`
- Tone: calm, confident, unhurried — not a sales pitch, a statement of fact
